Congratulations!

The greatest decision you can make is to give your heart and life to Jesus Christ. A relationship with Jesus secures your eternal future and leads to a fulfilling and fruitful life. God’s promise for your future is clearly revealed in His Word: “For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future” - Jeremiah 29:11

What's next?

To help you move forward in your new life in Christ, we encourage you to take the following practical steps of growth.

A New You Book

To discover and live out the wonderful plans God has for us, we must take seriously our responsibility to grow in the Lord by understanding and applying His Word. In this book, you'll get a good start on your journey toward spiritual growth and fruitfulness. The truths addressed here are essential in your walk with Christ and will lead to joy and amazing peace in your life!

Starting Point

God has an incredible purpose for your life! Over three Sundays in Starting Point, find out how to grow your relationship with God, become a vital part of His church, and make a real difference in the world around you.
